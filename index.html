Hello, I'm Oiza.

I'm a Web Developer based in Toronto.

JavaScript is my Jam

I have written code in modern ES6, React and Redux for startups. I've also written unit and integration tests in Jest and Enzyme, and recently began learning Gatsby and GraphQL. My earliest introduction to JavaScript was through Gordon Zhu's Watch and Code. That iterative process for solving problems continues to inform my approach to programming.

Front-end focused

I like making things pretty. The bootcamp and web fundamentals courses at Juno College (formerly HackerYou) went deep into advanced CSS, responsive design and accessibility principles. My relentless curiosity propels me to learn things like web typography (thank you Matej Latin!) and basic design principles to tune up this website (built mobile-first), and to help me work better with designers and product managers.

A global perspective

I discovered a love for building webpages while working in government relations at a UN agency. I think my lifelong exposure to diverse cultures makes me a uniquely well-rounded developer. You'll often find me at tech events & gatherings in my city, and avidly consuming news and debates on the latest practices and happenings in software development.
